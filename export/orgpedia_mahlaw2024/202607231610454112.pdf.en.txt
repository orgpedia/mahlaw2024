11. Selected trainee students will be assigned to officers in the Law and Justice Department, Legislative Branch.
92. Statement of Law Terms and Conditions of Internship Programme: -
(a) On being informed of the selection, the student will have to attend the internship site on the prescribed day and time at his / her own expense.
(b) The students selected under the internship programme will have to attend the office within the stipulated time by following all the rules of the office.
(c) The Secretary (Legal Statement) shall be bound to obey the orders of the officers of the Legislative Branch.
(d) The Secretary (Statement of Law) shall have the power to exclude from the selection process any candidate who at any stage of the selection process attempts in any manner to pressure the authorities concerned for selection.
(d) Students will be given provisional admit cards during the duration of the internship activity and it will be mandatory to submit these admit cards to the department after the end of the internship period.
(f) Students who have participated in any of the earlier batches of the internship activity will not be able to participate in this internship activity.
(g) If, for some unavoidable reason, it is not possible for the student to complete this activity during the internship period, the concerned student shall be required to give written advance notice to the Secretary (Statement of Law). Also, if it is not possible for the students to attend for some unavoidable reason, it will be mandatory for the concerned students to obtain prior intimation from the Secretary (Statement of Law).
(h) The Secretary (Statement of Law) shall have the power to cancel / terminate the internship of a student on grounds of indiscipline during the internship period.
(j) Every student shall be required to keep a daily record of the work done during the internship activity as well as the information gained from it.
(j) The Secretary (Statement of Law) shall have the power to modify the terms and conditions of the internship activity.
(k) The selected students will have to give an undertaking regarding the confidentiality of the work.
Work Report: - In the last week of completion of the internship activity, the trainee student will have to submit a report on the training to the Secretary (Legal Statement), Department of Law and Justice.
14. Certificate: - After successful completion of the internship activities by the students and submission of the work report, the students will be given a certificate of internship with the signature of the Secretary (Legal Statement), Department of Law & Justice.
15. Internship Scholarship: - Students who successfully complete the internship activities within the above prescribed time period will be paid Rs. A stipend of ₹10,000 / - (Rupees Ten Thousand only) will be given.
