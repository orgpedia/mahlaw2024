Introduction: Reference No. Under the Government Order herein, the Charity Association under the Department of Law and Justice has been reconstituted division-wise and under Annexure-A to the said Order, the officers of the concerned office have been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Charity Commissioner and the Joint Commissioner of the concerned charity as Controlling Officer for disbursement of funds to the offices of the charity organization on Budget Estimation, Allocation & Monitoring System (BEAMS) system. Accordingly, the grants received on BEAMS system from the Finance Department under the head "Demand No. J-02, 1970-Other Administrative Services, 00-Other Administrative Services, 001-Operation and Administration, (00) (03) -Departmental Staff of the Charity Commissioner (Mandatory) Dattamat (Ro\ SoAoW8)" for the expenditure incurred for the Divisional Offices under the Charity Commissioner at present is mutually distributed by the Budgetary Branch of the Department of Law and Justice to the concerned Controlling Officer.
However, it has been observed that grants disbursed to charitable Joint Commissioner Pune, Nashik, Kolhapur, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Latur, Amravati, Nagpur, and Nanded offices and their subordinate offices are not being spent on time and the grants are lying unutilized, resulting in non-receipt of grants or lack of grants to the offices which require grants. Therefore, in order to enforce financial discipline by maintaining proper coordination and financial control over the distribution of funds of the divisional and district level offices of the charitable organization, the Office of the Commissioner of Charities, Reference No. As per the proposal herein, the matter of devolution of fund distribution powers over the BEAMS system to the Charity Commissioner, State of Maharashtra was under consideration.

The Government Order:
The grants received on BEAMS system by the Finance Department under the head "Demand No. J-02,2070-Other Administrative Services, 00-Other Administrative Services, 001-Operation and Administration, (00) (03) -Departmental Staff of Charity Commissioners (Compulsory) Dattamat (20705049)" from the financial year 2026-27 for the expenses of the Divisional Offices of the Charitable Association in the State of Maharashtra under the Department of Law & Justice should be distributed to the Commissioner of Charities, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai (10061), the Controlling Officer.
The responsibility of further disbursement of the grant to the concerned Divisional / District Offices shall lie with the Controlling Officer, Charity Commissioner, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai (10061). Also, the Collective Budget Estimates, Revised Estimates, Supplementary Demands, Programme Budgets, Monthly Demands for Grants and other necessary information sought from the Department of Finance from time to time to the respective offices of the Department of Law and Justice; Coordination of Receipts, Deposits and Expenditure; and other related duties shall be the responsibility of the Commissioner of Charities, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai (J0061) as the Controlling Officer.
3. The said order. will be implemented from 01.04.2026.
